Feedback and Support

Any feedback you provide us with about this CBT package is valuable to us. If you have any comments or suggestions for improvements to the design or behaviour please let us know via email at pevesupport@cs.man.ac.uk. If the feedback you provide is specific to a particular CBT package, please state it's full course title. Thank you!

 

Support:

Course Related Problems

All problems related to understanding the course and its teaching material should be directed to your course tutor only.

Technical Problems

If you have encountered problems physically using the CBT package (e.g. navigation doesn't work), you should report such issues to pevesupport@cs.man.ac.uk. Please let us know:

  • The full course title of the CBT package.
  • What operating system you are using, e.g. Windows, Linux, Mac.
  • What browser you are using, e.g. Internet Explorer, Firefox, Mozilla, Seamonkey, Safari, Opera.
  • The version of the browser you are using.
    You can often find this out by looking for a menu option: Help > About...
  • And describe as much information as you can about the problem you are experiencing.

We will endeavour to get back to you as soon as we can.
